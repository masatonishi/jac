about

We are the first newly established fund
management company in Japanto apply
a buyout fund-type valuecreation approach
as a minority shareholder to a listed company.

Japan Activation Capital (“JAC”) supports Japanese listed companies achieve long-term, sustainable growth and providing value creation, based on mutual trust with the management team through investing a minority stake.

As a significant minority shareholder, we align ourselves with the portfolio companies and aim to elevate their businesses to a new stage. We backup companies’ bold decision making and support their execution on management initiatives to promote the company long-term and sustainable value. JAC comprises of seasoned professionals of various backgrounds, who have been involved in growth strategies of countless Japanese corporates. Our knowledge, experience and network are readily available to our portfolio companies.

message

Supporting the revival of Japan companies

Japanese economy and companies have long faced challenges from difficult macro environment that they are unable to control, such as rapidly changing geopolitical and macroeconomic risks, and persisting deflation and zero interest rate leading to depreciation of the currency and reduction in purchasing power. However, a company must always demonstrate its ability to navigate such macro headwind and achieve respectable results.

In our country, there is a noticeable number of companies and businesses that could be grossly underperforming their business potential and not utilizing their management and employees’ capabilities. It is essential for them to unlock the potential in a way visible to their business environment and capital markets. JAC is an investor that supports companies with management possessing strong will to achieve this, not just as a shareholder but as a committed partner to help execute their initiatives. Japan Activation Capital was established to help meeting the objective of creating a high sustainable growth, highly prosperous company together with the management. Once a strong foundation is established, the stock market will reward sustainable growth and earning potential, which in turn will influence other companies to follow and elevate the overall competitiveness of Japanese companies relative to global peers. We believe the collective effort of these companies will make a positive impact on the entire Japanese economy.

Japan Activation Capital wishes to support the “Activation” of committed Japanese companies and the entire Japanese economy.

Chief Executive Officer (Founder)Hiroyuki Otsuka

mission
& goal

Japan Activation Capital, through its funds, aims to support company management teams that are aspiring to elevate their businesses, instilling sustainable expectations for growth and persistent appreciation of market capitalization. Japan Activation Capital achieves this through constructive dialogue based on relationships of mutual trust, as well as by supporting the execution of strategic initiatives.

  • goal01

    Helping Japan's leading
    companies realize their potential

    Through our investments, we aim to increase the number of listed companies with a strong reputation for mid- to long-term growth and to ensure that these companies maintain a sustainable valuation that supports the appreciation of market capitalization

    Supporting portfolio companies raise the growth expectations of stakeholders (i.e. customers, employees, etc.) as well as capital markets.

  • goal02

    Restoring the good reputation
    of Japan's major companies

    We aim not only to transform our portfolio companies, but also to make a positive impact on their related industries and ultimately the entire Japanese economy.

    Through the growth of portfolio companies, extend the positive impact to related industries that could eventually lead to higher growth for the Japanese economy.

  • goal03

    Fostering a virtuous spiral
    throughout Japan society

    We aim to foster a virtuous economic cycle for the Society.

    On top of supporting the sustainability initiatives of our portfolio companies and utilizing digital solutions and HR/Organizational enhancement strategies, creating a virtuous cycle that benefits on the employees as well as SME business partners throughout the supply chains.

strategy

  • [ 01 ]

    Supporting the management of portfolio company as a significant minority shareholder

    To take a significant stake of a mid- to large-cap Tokyo Stock Exchange listed company exhibiting strong potential and support the management with defining and executing its initiatives

  • [ 02 ]

    Forming a true partnership with the management

    To execute a partnership agreement that enables constructive dialogue with the companies’ management aspiring to elevate their businesses, which is needed to help unlock their potential and accelerate growth

  • [ 03 ]

    Actively supporting value creation

    To create value using private equity value creation style support, not only by discussing strategies (‘What’ to do) but also by backing up their bold decision makings together and offering solutions and expertise to implement (“How” to do)

  • [ 04 ]

    Supporting the portfolio with a mid- to long-term view

    Providing support that fundamentally and sustainably improves the company’s growth profile without resorting to short-term solutions like share buybacks

  • [ 05 ]

    In Depth Research

    Conclude NDAs with investees and provide support for management reforms as an insider

approach

Japan Activation Capital was founded by an investment professional from a leading global private equity firm who has a strong track record of supporting the successful transformation of a number of Japanese companies. Together with senior professionals hailing from consulting firms, private and public equity funds, investment banks, and corporates, as well as senior executives from large corporations and financial institutions who have experience driving change as operating executives, senior advisors, and executive advisors, we intend to leverage our diverse knowledge base, experience, and broad network to provide high value-added solutions to portfolio companies.

Additionally, JAC Board of Directors and Investment Advisory Committee members are comprised of experienced professionals from a variety of backgrounds, who can provide an outside perspective on JAC’s strategy planning as well as management and investment decisions in order to maximize value added to our portfolio companies and maintain JAC’s management objectivity.

business

Reinforce execution ability required for business model upgrade to accelerate growth

  • In our country, we can observe many companies that are underweight by investors relative to their global peers. Some of the main reasons for this include overexpansion causing dilution of resources and excessive risk averseness in corporate culture. JAC as a stake holder supports the management concentrate its resources on core business, while strengthening the business itself, and improving profitability and cashflow generation capability through business portfolio reform.
  • Also, there is reluctance to deviate from an existing, historical successful business model, caused by inexperience in adjusting to changing business environment which restrains management’s speed. JAC offers its resources and expertise to address these issues, by acceleration of global expansion and new business development, business model upgrade, execution of M&A, and PMI support among other things. Additionally, our expert professionals in Digital Transformation, Human Resource development, and Sustainability work closely with the management team, as part of the holistic approach in supporting its growth acceleration.

sustainability

Upgrading business to grow along with Society and Environment

Companies must respond to risk associated with social, environmental issues, but also proactively seek new business opportunities that arise from it. Business that grows with society and environment (“Good Growth”) strengthens Japan’s industries and enriches our society. To accelerate Japanese companies’ “Good Growth”, JAC supports selection, preparation, and execution of initiatives required to enhance growth and corporate value, by offering abundance of expertise in sustainability and utilizing its global network.

dx

Upgrading the business model for the digital society era

Digital society is not only about pursuing efficiency through digital technology, but also using digital technology for business model transformation, including transformation to recurring model, service model, and data driven agile organization. On the other hand, many companies struggle to advance the initiatives because of lack of expertise, conflict with existing businesses, or concern over an unproven business. JAC utilizes rich resources and network to work together with management of portfolio companies to accelerate business transformation by planning and executing strategies around digital technology and creating value with people and organization.

ir

Communicating with capital markets and fostering high expectations from stakeholders

JAC supports portfolio companies nurture strong relationships with the capital markets by crafting appropriate and effective IR/SR dialogue, and helping develop new, long-term investor base. JAC’s professionals with a deep market knowledge support effective communication of management initiatives around growth strategies, that leads to higher expectations from the capital markets.

hr

Developing human resources and organization capable of driving global business management

At the root of managing human resources, individual’s self-actualization and growth potential play a critical role in corporate and economic activities, as part of the human centric philosophy. Maximizing and realizing individual’s potential is believed to contribute, not only to company’s success but to sustainable prosperity and well-being of society. Therefore, it is critical to maximize the use of human resources with a strong connection to the corporate philosophy and promote a “story” to help realize its vision. Moreover, to elevate individual’s growth and motivation, a fair and transparent evaluation and compensation system is an important foundation to support sustainable corporate transformation. JAC offers its full lineup of expert resources to portfolio companies committed to transformation, including recommending a balanced compensation scheme, and firmly backs their effort to build a highly competitive organization with talent management capabilities.
