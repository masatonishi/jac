about

We are the first newly established fund
management company in Japanto apply
a buyout fund-type valuecreation approach
as a minority shareholder to a listed company.

Japan Activation Capital (JAC) is a fund specializing in minority investments in major listed companies in Japan, and promotes long-term, sustainable value creation as a shareholder.
While taking risks as a shareholder, we support the bold decision-making and implementation of initiatives by managers with the intention of change.
JAC supports the enhancement of the added value of its portfolio companies with a diverse background mix of founders and CEOs from major foreign PEs, strategic consultants, PEs, investment banks, and business companies, as well as various management experience as advisors.

message

Supporting the revival of Japan companies

Despite their intention to change, there are many listed companies that have not taken action. I want to support such companies.
In terms of technology, human resources, and financial power, there is no difference between Japan and American companies. What makes the difference is the ability to execute management and the sense of speed.
We will close this gap and support the promotion of management reform with a sense of speed. If this can be done, expectations for the future of the company will increase, and the multiples used in the evaluation of corporate value and stock value will also increase.

Chief Executive Officer (Founder)Hiroyuki Otsuka

mission
& goal

Through the funds it manages, Japan Activation Capital aims to "achieve corporate growth and sustainable market capitalization growth with sustained expectations over the medium to long term through trust-based dialogue with investee management aiming for a new stage of growth and support for the implementation of management initiatives"

  • goal01

    Helping Japan's leading
    companies realize their potential

    Through investment, we aim to increase the number of companies that can achieve high stock prices through high stock valuation multiple with growth expectations and sustainability

    We support investee companies to evolve into companies that can make the stock market and stakeholders (customers and employees) feel a high sense of "expectation" for the future

  • goal02

    Restoring the good reputation
    of Japan's major companies

    We aim not only to transform the companies in which we invest, but also to have a healthy impact on the industry to which the company belongs and the entire Japan economy

    Through the transformation of our portfolio companies, we aim to have a healthy impact on the industries in which we invest, which in turn will lead to high growth of the Japan economy as a whole

  • goal03

    Fostering a virtuous spiral
    throughout Japan society

    We aim to foster a virtuous spiral for society as a whole.

    In addition to supporting sustainability activities at our portfolio companies, we encourage them to contribute to the company's employees and small and medium-sized business partners in the value chain, which will lead to the sustainability of the growth of the investee companies

strategy

  • [ 01 ]

    Boosting the potential of
    publicly traded companies

    Minority investment in listed companies that have not reached their potential Acquire about 5-10% of the shares of major listed companies in Japan and support investees as a major shareholder

  • [ 02 ]

    Supporting a management team
    with a strong desire to change

    While taking risks as a shareholder, we support bold decision-making and the implementation of initiatives by managers with the intention of change

  • [ 03 ]

    Active support for value creation

    Value creation through a buyout fund approach
    Hold discussions with management on strategy ("What to do") and support execution ("How to do it")

  • [ 04 ]

    Long-term growth

    Have a medium- to long-term vision (what the company will look like in 10 years) and act as an opinion leader for shareholders

  • [ 05 ]

    In Depth Research

    Conclude NDAs with investees and provide support for management reforms as an insider

approach

Our company was founded by a former member of a major global private equity fund with experience in helping numerous Japan companies transform, and includes former strategic consulting firms, private equity funds, publicly traded equity funds, investment banks, and business companies. Operating executives and senior advisors with experience in leading transformations as management teams at major operating companies and financial institutions will gather to pursue the provision of high added value to investees through their diverse knowledge and networks

In addition, the Company's directors, members of the Investment Advisory Committee, and advisors are also external experts with a number of achievements, and by incorporating outside knowledge into the Company's strategic planning, management decisions, and investment decisions, the Company will provide maximum added value to its portfolio companies and conduct management with the objectivity of the Company

business

As a major shareholder, we support listed companies in Japan that have management teams that intend to transform into companies that can generate a long-term, sustainable sense of expectation, through medium- to long-term minority investments

  • We invest in major and medium-sized companies listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange
  • Based on a relationship of trust with management with a strong will for change and growth, we not only discuss strategies and other matters, but also support the implementation of management initiatives
  • We do not propose short-term measures to improve the stock price, such as share buybacks, but support the risk-taking of the management team so that bold management initiatives can be taken with the aim of "substantial and sustainable corporate growth" and "sustainable improvement of shareholder value accompanied by an increase in multiple valuations in the stock market (a sense of expectation from shareholders)"

sustainability

Companies need to respond to environmental and social risks and proactively respond to new business opportunities that arise around them. Good Growth, a business that grows together with the environment and society, will strengthen Japan's industry in the medium to long term and create a prosperous society. JAC will provide the following support to support and accelerate the Good Growth of Japan companies.

  • Formulation of a clear sustainability policy with an eye on medium- to long-term trends
  • Selection and concentration of sustainability initiatives that contribute to business growth and enhancement of corporate value
  • Coordination with brand strategy and IR strategy
  • Incentive design and implementation system to implement the plan
